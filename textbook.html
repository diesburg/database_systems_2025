Directions to sign up for the textbook:

  • Sign in or create an account at http://learn.zybooks.com
  • Enter zyBook code: UNICS3140_5140DiesburgFall2025

If you have taken this class before, you may be able to get a subscription to your textbook for free or a reduced cost! Please see this FAQ for directions.

It's easiest to buy the textbook directly from zyBooks without going to the bookstore. However, if you need to purchase all your textbooks through the UNI bookstore for financial aid reasons, you can buy a pre-paid access key from the UNI bookstore. Then, when you sign into learn.zybooks.com, you will need both the zyBook code and the pre-paid access key to gain access to the textbook. See this FAQ for directions on how to enter the pre-paid access key.

You will be graded on doing assigned participation and challenge activities, starting on the first week of class!
